# 🎣 Phishing Awareness Training

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains a presentation on **Phishing Awareness Training**, created to educate users about phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and cybersecurity best practices.

The presentation covers:
- What phishing is
- Different types of phishing attacks
- How to recognize phishing emails
- Identifying fake websites
- Social engineering tactics used by attackers
- Best practices to stay protected
- Real-world phishing examples
- Interactive quiz for learning

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

After viewing this presentation, you will be able to:

- Understand what phishing attacks are.
- Identify common phishing emails and fake websites.
- Recognize social engineering techniques.
- Apply cybersecurity best practices.
- Respond appropriately to phishing attempts.
